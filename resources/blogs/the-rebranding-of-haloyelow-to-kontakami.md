## Kontakami Products
With the launch of Kontakami Products, we are introducing innovative solutions designed to enhance your business communication experience and better meet customer needs:

### Contact Center Software
This solution helps businesses manage customer communication effectively across various media, such as phone, email, and chat. The integration of media ensures that each interaction runs smoothly and responsively, improving operational efficiency. This enables teams to respond to customer requests faster, making customers feel more valued and satisfied, which ultimately increases their loyalty to the brand.

### Inbound Contact Center Solution
This solution is designed to improve speed and efficiency in handling customer requests. With intuitive features and structured workflows, customer service teams can respond to inquiries and issues more quickly, reducing wait times and enhancing customer satisfaction.

### Outbound Contact Center
Designed to extend business reach, the Outbound Contact Center utilizes SIP technology and innovative features to boost sales and customer loyalty. With an efficient system, teams can make targeted marketing calls, while using analytics to assess campaign effectiveness and optimize strategies.

### Omnichannel Contact Center
The Omnichannel Contact Center connects businesses with customers across various platforms, including WhatsApp, email, social media, and phone. This approach creates a consistent and integrated experience, enabling quick responses that enhance customer satisfaction and strengthen relationships.

### Messaging - Live Chat, WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, and Email
The messaging feature allows for direct interaction with customers on popular platforms. With live chat and social media, customers can quickly get information and solutions, increasing satisfaction and loyalty through a more interactive experience.

### Voice - Web Call and SIP Call
The Voice solution includes web call and SIP call, which utilize internet networks to reduce communication costs and improve call quality. This system ensures flexibility, allowing agents to connect with customers from anywhere.

### Self-Service Contact Center
The Self-Service Contact Center enables customers to get assistance without direct interaction with an agent. An intuitive portal allows them to quickly find answers, improving efficiency and satisfying customers who prefer self-service solutions.

### Ticketing Management System
The Ticketing Management System efficiently records and manages customer requests. With an organized system, each issue can be handled promptly, ensuring responsive service and a better customer experience.

### ACD, IVR, CTI, Preview Dialer, and Call Recording
Features such as ACD, IVR, CTI, preview dialer, and call recording support better call management. These features improve service quality with analytics and agent training and provide insights into customer interactions to improve service processes.
